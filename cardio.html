Cardio: Walking vs Running

Cardio Showdown: Walking vs Running

Overview

Walking and running are both effective forms of cardiovascular exercise. They help improve heart health, burn calories, and reduce stress. But which one is better for you?

Comparison

Aspect Walking Running
Calories Burned (30 min) 120–150 300–450
Joint Impact Low High
Injury Risk Minimal Moderate to High
Accessibility Very Easy Moderate
Speed of Fitness Gains Slower Faster

Which Should You Choose?

  • Choose walking if you’re new to exercise, have joint issues, or prefer low-impact movement.
  • Choose running if you want faster results and can tolerate the intensity.
  • Mix both for variety and sustainability in your routine!
